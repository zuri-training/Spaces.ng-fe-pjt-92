HubOnze

HubOnze is a top-class workspace that lies in a harrow area in Lagos at plot 1 Orimolade Street, Ikeja, Lagos, Nigera.

It boasts a tastefully furnished waiting room with free Wi-fi internet access and flat screen TV. Available Offices are booked at different costs.

A variety of local and international dishes are also available at the restaurant (and of course , coffee!). On-site parking is allowed and is security is assured.
